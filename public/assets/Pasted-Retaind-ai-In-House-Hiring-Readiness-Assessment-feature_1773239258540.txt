Retaind.ai In-House Hiring Readiness Assessment feature
Build a web-based In-House Hiring Readiness Assessment feature for Retaind.ai.
Position this 
The system must diagnose structural weaknesses across the recruitment lifecycle and show how Retaind.ai enables evidence-led hiring and stronger decision quality.
The diagnostic must align to The Recruitment Failure Map™ and the 10 lifecycle failure points, including:
1 Requirement Capture
2 Job Advert Writing
3 Employer Branding
4 Passive Talent Sourcing
5 Job Advertising Strategy
6 Employer Value Communication
7 Behavioural Benchmarking
8 Candidate Assessment
9 Interview Strategy
10 Evidence-Led Hiring Decisions
The platform must:
1 Present a landing page
2 Deliver the hiring readiness questionnaire
3 Capture lead information
4 Calculate a Hiring Readiness Score (0-100)
5 Diagnose hiring maturity category
6 Identify Top 5 structural weakness areas
7 Generate a personalised strategy report
8 Include The Recruitment Failure Map visual
9 Include Hiring Improvement Opportunity section
10 Store responses for analytics
11 Encourage users to book demo / create free Retaind account
The experience must feel like a consulting-grade hiring diagnostic, not a marketing quiz.
