REPLIT DESIGN PROMPT
Retaind Assessment Pathway Selector Page

Create a new landing page inside the Retaind.ai site that appears after the main homepage and before either assessment begins.

This page should act as a pathway selection page helping visitors choose the most relevant scorecard experience for their situation.

The page must be visually polished, conversion-focused, and aligned to a premium SaaS / consulting-grade design standard.

Do not make this page feel like a basic navigation screen. It should feel like a strategic “choose your path” experience.

PAGE PURPOSE

The page must help visitors immediately self-identify as one of two audience types:

1. Agency Recruiter

For recruiters, agency owners, and retained search businesses who want to:

win more exclusive / retained campaigns

improve recruiter positioning

scale revenues

deliver structured evidence-based shortlists

2. In-House Recruiter / Hiring Team

For internal recruiters, HR leaders, hiring managers, founders, and leadership teams who want to:

improve hiring accuracy

strengthen role definition

attract better candidates

reduce mis-hire risk

create a more evidence-led hiring process

The page should increase relevance and conversion by directing users into the correct assessment funnel.

PAGE DESIGN GOAL

Design a premium intermediate landing page called:

Choose Your Hiring Growth Path

Sub-headline:

Retaind.ai supports both agency recruiters and in-house hiring teams — select the assessment that best fits your role to get the most relevant strategy insights.

The page should visually communicate that the two pathways solve different but related problems.

LAYOUT STRUCTURE

Use a clean modern layout with:

1. Hero section
2. Two large audience pathway cards
3. Short “Why this matters” section
4. Optional reassurance / trust strip
5. Bottom CTA / guidance section

The layout should feel simple, premium, and highly intuitive.

HERO SECTION

Headline:

Choose Your Assessment Path

Sub-headline:

Whether you’re building retained recruitment revenue as an agency recruiter or improving hiring quality as an in-house team, Retaind.ai will guide you to the right next step.

Short supporting text:

Select the assessment that best matches your role to receive a tailored strategy report, practical recommendations, and the most relevant Retaind.ai pathway.

Optional microcopy:

Estimated completion time: 2–4 minutes

MAIN PATHWAY SECTION

Display two large side-by-side cards on desktop and stacked cards on mobile.

Each card should be highly clickable and act as the main CTA.

CARD 1 — AGENCY RECRUITER PATH

Title:

I’m an Agency Recruiter

Subtitle:

For recruiters, agency owners, and search firms

Icon / visual direction:

Use an icon or illustration suggesting:

growth

commercial performance

retained search

recruiter transformation

Card copy:

Discover how ready you are to transition from contingency to structured retained recruitment, increase your fees, improve client positioning, and unlock higher-value search campaigns.

Include bullet points:

Measure your retained recruitment readiness

Identify growth barriers and commercial opportunities

See your revenue potential through retained campaigns

Get a personalised recruiter strategy brief

Primary CTA button:

Start Agency Recruiter Assessment

Secondary micro-link:

Best for agency recruiters, solo recruiters, team leaders, and agency owners

CARD 2 — IN-HOUSE RECRUITER PATH

Title:

I’m an In-House Hiring Leader

Subtitle:

For internal recruiters, HR leaders, hiring managers, and founders

Icon / visual direction:

Use an icon or illustration suggesting:

structure

hiring process

team alignment

decision quality

evidence-led hiring

Card copy:

Assess the strength of your hiring process, identify structural weaknesses across the recruitment lifecycle, and discover how to improve hiring accuracy, shortlist quality, and decision confidence.

Include bullet points:

Diagnose weak points in your hiring lifecycle

Identify your top structural hiring risks

See your hiring improvement opportunity

Get a personalised hiring strategy brief

Primary CTA button:

Start In-House Hiring Assessment

Secondary micro-link:

Best for talent acquisition teams, HR leaders, hiring managers, and growth-stage businesses

CARD DESIGN REQUIREMENTS

Each card must feel premium and high-conversion.

Design guidance:

rounded corners

subtle shadow

generous padding

clear hierarchy

hover state with lift / border emphasis

large CTA button

entire card clickable

strong contrast between the two options

Use a visual style that feels more like premium SaaS onboarding than a traditional website tile.

WHY THIS MATTERS SECTION

Add a short section below the cards:

Headline:

Two Different Hiring Challenges. One Smarter System.

Body copy:

Agency recruiters and in-house hiring teams face different commercial and operational challenges.

Agency recruiters need stronger positioning, better campaign structure, and tools to win and deliver retained recruitment.

In-house teams need better role definition, stronger candidate attraction, more structured evaluation, and greater confidence in hiring decisions.

Retaind.ai supports both journeys with structured workflows, behavioural benchmarking, evidence-led assessment, and smarter hiring tools.

OPTIONAL TRUST / REASSURANCE STRIP

Add a narrow reassurance band with 3–4 short statements such as:

Tailored assessment results

Strategy-led recommendations

Built for real-world recruitment teams

Takes just a few minutes

If metrics are available later, leave this area easy to update with:

number of assessments completed

average improvement insights

client / recruiter trust signals

BOTTOM GUIDANCE SECTION

Headline:

Not Sure Which Path Fits You?

Body copy:

Choose the path that reflects the role you currently play most often.

If you are an agency recruiter selling and delivering recruitment services to clients, choose the Agency Recruiter Assessment.

If you are responsible for hiring within your own organisation, choose the In-House Hiring Assessment.

Optional support link:

Need help choosing? Contact us

UX REQUIREMENTS

This page must:

sit between homepage and assessment start

clearly route users to the correct assessment

minimise confusion

increase conversion through relevance

work perfectly on mobile and desktop

The card CTA should route to:

/assessment/agency-recruiter

/assessment/in-house-hiring

or equivalent routes used in the app

VISUAL DESIGN DIRECTION

Style the page to match a premium B2B SaaS brand.

Design cues:

modern clean typography

strong whitespace

subtle gradients or soft background sections

restrained use of brand colours

professional iconography

polished CTA buttons

Do not make the page busy or overly corporate.
It should feel:

strategic

intelligent

premium

frictionless

MOTION / INTERACTION

Add subtle interaction enhancements:

hover animation on cards

CTA button state changes

slight card elevation on hover

smooth transitions

Do not overdo motion.
Keep it elegant and fast.

MOBILE DESIGN

On mobile:

stack the two cards vertically

keep CTAs large and thumb-friendly

maintain strong spacing

keep all copy concise and readable

ACCESSIBILITY

Ensure:

strong text contrast

clear heading hierarchy

keyboard accessible cards / buttons

semantic HTML

screen reader friendly CTA labels

OPTIONAL ENHANCEMENT

If practical, add a very small selector at the top:

What best describes you today?

Agency recruiter

In-house hiring team

When selected, smoothly scroll / highlight the relevant card.

This is optional but can improve engagement.

CONTENT TONE

The copy should feel:

commercially intelligent

supportive

high-value

clear and concise

Avoid jargon-heavy wording.
Avoid making either route feel secondary.

Both paths should feel equally important and professionally positioned.

SUCCESS CRITERIA

The page is successful if:

users instantly understand which path is right for them

the two assessments feel distinct and relevant

click-through rate into each assessment increases

the user experience feels polished and premium

the page strengthens trust before the assessment begins

IMPLEMENTATION NOTE

This page must be added as an additional step in the assessment flow:

Homepage → Pathway Selector Page → Chosen Assessment → Lead Capture → Results Report

Do not bypass this page unless a visitor comes directly from a campaign link that is already audience-specific.

OPTIONAL FUTURE UPGRADE

Keep the layout flexible enough to support this in future.
